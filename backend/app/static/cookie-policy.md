## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, phone, tablet) when you visit websites.

**What cookies do:**
- Remember your preferences
- Keep you logged in
- Analyze how you use the site
- Help us improve your experience

**What cookies DON'T do:**
- ❌ Install viruses or malware
- ❌ Access your files
- ❌ Steal your identity

---

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### 2.1 Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies **cannot be disabled** — they are necessary for the website to function.

| Cookie Name     | Purpose                  | Duration |
|-----------------|--------------------------|----------|
| `session_token` | Keep you logged in       | Session (cleared when browser closes) |
| `csrf_token`    | Prevent security attacks | Session |
| `user_id`       | Identify your account    | Session |
| `auth_refresh`  | Refresh your login       | 7 days  |

**Legal Basis:** Necessary for service provision (GDPR Article 6(1)(b))

### 2.2 Functional Cookies (Optional)

These cookies remember your preferences and **can be controlled** by you.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `language_preference` | Remember your language choice | 1 year |
| `theme_mode` | Remember dark/light mode | 1 year |
| `currency_preference` | Remember JPY/LKR display | 1 year |
| `cookie_consent` | Remember your cookie choices | 1 year |

**Legal Basis:** Legitimate interest (GDPR Article 6(1)(f))
**Control:** Cookie Preferences (banner or Settings)

### 2.3 Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us understand how users interact with our site and **can be controlled** by you.

**Google Analytics:**

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `_ga` | Distinguish users | 2 years |
| `_gid` | Distinguish users | 24 hours |
| `_gat` | Throttle request rate | 1 minute |

**What we track:**
- Pages visited
- Time spent on site
- Browser/device type
- Geographic location (city level)
- Traffic sources

**What we DON'T track:**
- Personally identifiable information
- Exact location (GPS)
- Keystrokes or passwords

**Legal Basis:** Consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a))
**Control:** Cookie Preferences or Google Analytics Opt-Out

### 2.4 Marketing Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us show relevant content and measure ad effectiveness. They **can be controlled** by you.

**Facebook Pixel:**
- `_fbp` — Track conversions (90 days)
- Used for ad targeting on Facebook

**Google Ads:**
- `_gcl_*` — Track ad clicks (90 days)
- Used to measure ad effectiveness

**What we use them for:**
- Show you relevant vehicle recommendations
- Measure advertising effectiveness
- Avoid showing you ads you've already seen

**Legal Basis:** Consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a))
**Control:** Cookie Preferences

## 4. How to Control Cookies

### 4.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit ClearDrive.lk, you will see a cookie banner with options:
- **Accept All** — Allow all cookies (recommended)
- **Reject Non-Essential** — Only essential cookies
- **Customize** — Choose specific categories

### 4.2 Cookie Preferences (Anytime)

You can change your cookie preferences at any time.

**How:** Settings → Privacy → Cookie Preferences

Choose from:
- ✅ **Essential** (always on — cannot be disabled)
- ☐ Functional
- ☐ Analytics
- ☐ Marketing

### 4.3 Browser Settings

You can control cookies directly in your browser:

| Browser     | How to Manage Cookies                                 |
|-------------|-------------------------------------------------------|
| **Chrome**  | Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies             |
| **Firefox** | Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data |
| **Safari**  | Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data           |
| **Edge**    | Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions               |

> **Note:** Blocking essential cookies will prevent the website from functioning properly.

### 4.4 Opt-Out Tools

- **Google Analytics Opt-Out:** https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
- **Do Not Track (DNT):** We respect the DNT browser setting for analytics cookies
- **Your Online Choices:** http://www.youronlinechoices.com/
- **Network Advertising Initiative:** http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/
